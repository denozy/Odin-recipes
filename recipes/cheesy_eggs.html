Cheesy Scrambled Egss

Cheesy eggs is an excellent breakfast for any occassion. Eggs are filled with protein, and cheese is just plain tasty, a match made in heaven.

Ingredients

  • Eggs 1-4 dependings on your appetite. Maybe even more, don't let me tell you how many eggs to eat.
  • Cheese - any kind, thick shredded
  • milk(optional) 1tbsp
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter

Directions

  1. Break eggs into mixing bowl
  2. If you like them fluffy, add some milk
  3. whisk with fork
  4. Add mixture to hot buttered pain
  5. Add salt and pepper to taste
  6. Add cheese, as much as you want, go crazy
  7. Using a silcon spatula, scramble them eggs to your desired consistency, I like a rough scramble personally.
  8. When properly scrambled and cooked(about 2 minutes, variable based on your pan heat), plate and enjoy!
