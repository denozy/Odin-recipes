Pasta with Red Sauce

This simplest and one of the most delicious recipes out there. Pasta with red sauce, or Spaghetti Bolognase

Ingredients

  • Box of pasta
  • Jar of Sauce
  • .5lb 93% ground beef
  • Onion 1 Small
  • Green Bell pepper
  • Parmesean Cheese

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil
  2. Add dry pasta to water and follow directions on box for timing
  3. dice onion and pepper
  4. In a sauce pan, add olive oil and cook diced vegetables
  5. Remove diced vegatables and set aside
  6. Add a bit more olive oil, then cook ground beef, breaking it down to small pieces
  7. Once the beef is fully cooked, add veggies back
  8. Add red sauce of choice and bring to a simmer
  9. Enjoy, best topped with parmesean cheese!
