Chicken and Rice Meal Prep

This delicious chicken and rice based meal prep is nutritious and full of flavor. It's a recipe I've enjoyed for years and am yet to grow tired of, i'm sure you'll enjoy it too!

Ingredients

  • Jasmine Rice - 6 cups
  • Chicken Breast - 6
  • Onion - 2
  • Bell Pepper (any color) - 3
  • Black beans 2 16oz cans
  • Kidney beans 1 16oz can
  • Garbonzo beans 1 16oz can
  • Chicken Bouillon 1.5tbsp
  • Spices
    • Chili Powder 3tbsp
    • Paprika 3tbsp
    • Onion powder 1tbsp
    • Garlic Powder 1tbsp
    • Oregano 1tbsp
    • Cumin 1/2tbsp
    • Red pepper flakes 1/2tbsp
    • Salt 1tsp
    • Ground black pepper 1tsp
    • Bay leaves 3

Directions

  1. Prepare chicken for the slow cooker
    1. Use a fork to poke holes all throughout chicken breasts
    2. Trim undesirable pieces from the chicken breasts
    3. Pour 6 cups of water into slow cooker
    4. Add bouillon to water
    5. Combine all spices and add to slow cooker
    6. Add chicken to slow cooker
    7. Set slow cooker to high and cook for 6 hours
  2. Prepare Rice
    1. Add 10 cups or 60floz of water to a large pot
    2. Using a mesh strainer, wash your rice thoroughly until the water runs clear
    3. Add rinced rice to still cold water
    4. In the uncovered pot, bring rice to a boil for one minute, then cover and leave to simmer for 15. For best results, do not uncover pot
  3. Prepare Veggies
    1. Add olive oil to pan
    2. Dice Onions and Peppers to desired size
    3. Add onions to pan 3 minutes before peppers
    4. Add peppers and sautee for 5 minutes
    5. Lower heat and add minced garlic, allow to lightly simmer for 2 more minutes
  4. Prepare Beans
    1. Open and rinse beans using a mesh strainer
    2. Combine all beans in a mixing bowl
  5. Combine Ingredients
    1. Using air tight containers of your choice, portion rice into each
    2. Layer beans atop rice distributing them evenly across meal preps
    3. Layer sauted veggies atop meal prep
    4. Add desired sauces
    5. Layer chicken
  6. Seal your meal prep containers and store them in the fridge or freezer for future consumption! This recipe should make 14 meals!
